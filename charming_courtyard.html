Courtyard Cottage

We believe everyday is a good day to be pampered!

Larger than the Cozy Cottage this cottage is perfect for couples that like a little bit more room and not quite as many flowery things in the decor.

Beautiful, roomy cottage with windows and views galore! Huge picture window overlooking our wooded hollow and another window looking out onto the courtyard terrace.

French provincial decor with mahogany style furniture, queen size bed with poster headboard, comfortable sitting area with love seat and coffee table.

The cottage is also equipped with microwave, refrigerator, iron, ironing board and radio alarm clock. Bathroom with shower, hairdryer.

Watch the squirrels chase each other (14 black walnut trees attract plenty of squirrels - there even is a flying one!) Listen to the birds and delight in the sighting of our resident groundhog and numerous deer roaming in the hollow.
