This charming, one room cottage has two queen size beds, a comfortable sitting area with two wicker chairs, a desk with chair and is the perfect cottage for couples, families and parties up to four people.

The cottages is also equipped with microwave, refrigerator, iron, ironing board, radio alarm clock and boasts a huge picture window with a wonderful view of our wooded hollow. Bathroom with shower, hairdryer.

This cottage sits at the end of the driveway, so it's nice, quiet and very private. Easy, reserved parking is right in front of your door. A large stone patio with a picnic table right next to cottage invites you to sit and enjoy the beautiful setting.
