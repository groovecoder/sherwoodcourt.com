Patio Delight Cottage

We believe your surroundings can affect your dreams!

This is a two-bedroom cottage which is perfect for two, three or four people traveling together or for families with children. (The kids always love having their own little room!)

Discount Rate:
Sunday -Thursday* $99

Weekend Rate:
Friday-Saturday* $109

 Reserve Now 

The larger room is furnished with one queen bed, and a comfy sitting area with love seat and coffee table. There is a doorway that leads to the second, smaller room which has a full-sized bed.

The cottage is also equipped with microwave, refrigerator, iron, ironing board and radio alarm clock.

Bathroom with tub/shower combination, hairdryer. Bright and airy with lots of windows. Beautiful view of the hollow in the back. The side window overlooks the large, flower framed flagstone patio right next to the cottage.

The fire pit is a delightful place to sit and enjoy a cup of coffee in the early morning, a picnic at lunch, or a glass of wine in the evening.
