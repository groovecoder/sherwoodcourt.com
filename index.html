Welcome to Sherwood Court - a charming Ozark oasis!

Escape to Sherwood Court and experience unsurpassed hospitality. Relax and enjoy the beauty of the award-winning flower gardens that are found scattered throughout the property.

Innkeepers Bryan and Lil Hostick are the proud owners of this charming Ozark oasis. The property has been lovingly restored, remodeled and updated. Sherwood Court sets the highest standard for cleanliness in a quaint and relaxing setting. Anchored by it's courtyard, situated at the neck of a beautiful wooded ozark hollow, Sherwood Court truly is a very special and unique place to visit.

Sherwood Court is not your typical, run of the mill hotel. Nor is it a high priced, frilly Bed and Breakfast!

This nostalgic motor court, one of the first and last to exist in Eureka Springs, offers old-fashioned charm, reminiscent of quieter times. Enhanced with modern conveniences, a visit to Sherwood Court will send you home refreshed and rejuvenated!

FREE wireless high-speed internet is available in all of the accommodations at Sherwood Court.

Bring your laptop and check your email or surf the internet in the comfort of your room or cottage, or in the beauty of one of the many patio areas.

Located in the Historic District, Sherwood Court is only one half mile from downtown with shopping and restaurants galore, and only minutes from the Great Passion Play and many other attractions.

Conveniently located directly on the Trolley line, your vacation can be hassle free with no traffic or parking worries!

Accomodations include seven charming cottages (all uniquely furnished and decorated), and four motel rooms (two singles – two doubles).

From simple to elaborate, Sherwood Court offers something for everyone and every budget. Please come and experience this charming Ozark oasis. You will be glad you did!
