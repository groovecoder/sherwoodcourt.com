If atmosphere is what you desire and frilly pink stuff and Victorian Lace are not your style, then it doesn't get any better than our charming, 2-room Log Cabin.

Light the fireplace, put on your favorite CD, pop some popcorn in the microwave, take the bubbly out of the fridge, sink into your red, heart-shaped Jacuzzi tub and leave the world behind. Just you and your sweetheart.
Now, it doesn't get anymore romantic than that!
You will never want to leave.

Mini kitchen area with microwave and refrigerator, Stereo with CD player.
Features also include iron, ironing board and radio alarm clock.
Wrap around windows with the most wonderful view of our wooded hollow.
Separate bathroom with shower, hairdryer.

The original restored Log Cabin from the late 1800's offers a queen-sized wrought iron bed with love dove head board that looks onto the fireplace (gas log) and two rocking chairs to sit by the fire.

Private, flower framed, covered front porch with a cedar settee to sit, relax, and watch the world go by.
