Weddings & Honeymoons

We believe weddings don't have to cost a fortune to be memorable!

We would be delighted to be a part of this most special time in your lives and to help make it a wonderful memory and a stress and hassle free experience for you.

We specialize in small, sweet and simple, informal and inexpensive weddings performed in a romantic garden setting. Just the two of you and a minister to officiate.

Whether you choose our cedar gazebo in the hollow under a canopy of trees or a tucked away, flower framed stone patio complete with arbor, our award winning grounds are the perfect setting for an outdoor wedding. If the weather is not cooperating, we can easily move the ceremony inside your cottage or the office.

We can also easily take care of the Bridal Bouquet, Wedding Cake, Toasting Champagne, or any other extras you may desire!

We provide the photographer as well!
You are welcome to bring your own camera and we will take the pictures for you at no extra charge!

Of course we can also arrange for a professional Wedding Photographer to capture your Wedding Day in pictures. Price varies according to types and amount of pictures.

Whatever your budget , whichever location and package you choose your special day is sure to be a memorable one.

Stay for a three night honeymoon and we will give you a free wedding ceremony!

Arkansas Marriage License Information:

To get a marriage license in Arkansas, each individual must be over 18 years old to get married without parental consent. No blood test or waiting period is involved. You must show proper ID. The forms may be filled out at any county courthouse in the state. After the ceremony, the license is to be turned back into the County Clerk's office for registration.

The County Clerk's office hours in Eureka Springs are from 8:30 a.m. - 4:00 p.m., Monday through Friday; and on Saturday from 9:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m., except on holidays, weather permitting.
A marriage license costs $58.00 (cash only, exact change)
